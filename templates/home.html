Grumble

are all your friends indecisive about where to eat? too scared to seem pushy? then use Grumble to solve your problems!
one person of your group can fill out a survey and Grumble will compile places to eat that fit the requirements.
a code will generate that can be entered by the rest of the group to start the matching process. say yes, no, or heart
each restaurant shown. Grumble will then find the most wanted restaurant for the indecisive group. happy eating!
